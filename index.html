Volcanoes

Due to the recent volcano eruptions in Hawaii and Guatemala, volcanoes have once again become a popular subject. Questions are raised how such an eruption can be predicted and what are relevant factors to consider. One of these factors is the type of volcano. This data visualisation will provide insight in different types of volcanoes and will attempt to answer the question whether the type is correlated with the number of eruptions. This is done by visualizing a world map that takes into account all the countries in which a volcano has erupted. With a click on a certain country, a donut chart is provided which illustrates all the types of volcanoes within the selected country. Clicking on a specific type in the donut chart, a scatterplot will appear that shows all the eruptions of that type that occurred.
